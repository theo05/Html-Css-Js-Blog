Sports

As somebody who has gone deep down the rabbit-hole of nutrition for cramps in sport, in a bid to help give my clients and edge over their competition, I can confirm that cramps are a complex topic. There is a lot that is not known. There is a lot that does not work. And there are also a lot of people who try to simplify it down to one thing, as if one specific strategy will solve cramps for all athletes. It is clear that cramps are multifactorial. And I would wager that in the coming decades, we will probably have better solutions than we have now. Cramps are also hard to study.
